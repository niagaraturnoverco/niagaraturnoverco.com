Core services

What We Provide

Three distinct service types — not one-size-fits-all. The right level depends on your booking volume and how hands-off you want to be.

On-Demand Coverage
Emergency backup when your regular cleaner cancels or no-shows. Same-day response, no gaps, no excuses. Best for hosts who want a reliable backup without a recurring commitment.
  • Same-day availability
  • Guest-ready turnover reset
  • Photo confirmation every job
  • No commitment required
Operational Coverage
Recurring turnovers handled consistently every week — synced to your calendar, without you managing every detail. This is the core service most active hosts use. Includes backup crew by default.
  • Priority scheduling
  • Recurring weekly coverage
  • Laundry and linen reset
  • Property-specific checklist
  • Backup crew on standby
Full System Management
Operations handled at the system level for multi-unit operators. Custom workflow, restocking, performance reporting, and peak season priority access. Quoted individually after reviewing your setup.
  • Multi-unit coordination
  • Restocking and oversight
  • Custom workflow support
  • Monthly performance report
  • Peak season priority access
Every single turnover

What's Included in Every Job

This isn't a checklist you have to send us. It's built into the standard for every turnover we complete.

Full room reset
Every bedroom, bathroom, kitchen, and living area cleaned and reset to guest-ready standard.
Photo confirmation
Photos of every room sent to your phone after the job — before your guest checks in. Automatic, every time.
Backup crew on standby
Every job has a backup plan. If coverage is ever threatened, the backup is already in place — not being organized at the last minute.
On-time execution
Turnovers are scheduled within your checkout-to-check-in window. No delays, no asking you to push the check-in time.
Property-specific checklist
Built around your unit, not a generic template. Every quirk, every host preference, accounted for.
Clear communication
If something is off at the property — guest damage, missing item — you're flagged immediately with photos. No hiding problems.
Service scope

What We Do. What We Don't.

Being clear about scope is how we protect quality. We'd rather tell you what we don't do than overpromise and underdeliver.

We handle
  • Guest-ready cleaning and reset
  • Linen and towel turnover
  • Trash removal and restocking essentials
  • Laundry coordination (Operational tier+)
  • Same-day and emergency coverage
  • Photo documentation after every job
  • Property condition flagging
  • Multi-unit coordination (System tier)
Out of scope
  • Exterior property maintenance
  • Appliance repairs or plumbing
  • Guest communication or check-in management
  • Listing management or pricing strategy
  • Properties outside Niagara Region

If you're unsure whether we cover your situation — text us. We'll give you a straight answer.
