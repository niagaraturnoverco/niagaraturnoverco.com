Pricing variables

What changes the final price.

The ranges above cover the standard case. Three things push the price toward the top end.

Laundry service
Basic wash: +$20. Full service (wash, dry, fold, stage): +$40.
Unit condition
Heavy use or extended stays add time. Condition after checkout affects final cost.
Turnaround speed
Same-day: +20%. Emergency (2hr notice): +40%.
Amenities
Hot tub: +$25. BBQ/outdoor kitchen: +$15. Pet-friendly units: +$20.
